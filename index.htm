Jia Li (李嘉)

 jiali.gm@gmail.com

 

I am a Boya postdoctoral fellow (July, 2018-June, 2020) at Peking University working with Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Chao Xu.

 

I received my Ph.D. in computer science in 2017 from Beijing Jiaotong University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Jian Yu.

 

My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, image processing, and computational photography.
