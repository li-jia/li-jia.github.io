I am now a Lecturer in the School of Artificial Intelligence, Beijing Normal University.

 

From July 2018 to June 2020, I worked as a Boya postdoctoral researcher at Peking University working with Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Chao Xu. I was also a member of the ZERO Lab at Peking University.

 

I received my Ph.D. degree in computer science in 2017 from Beijing Jiaotong University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Jian Yu.

 

My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, image processing, and computational photography.
